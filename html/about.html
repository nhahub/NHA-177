About AI Fitness Trainer

Your Smart Health & Fitness Prediction Assistant

Project Overview

AI Fitness Trainer provides personalized fitness and nutrition recommendations using your input data. The platform calculates daily nutrients (calories, protein, carbs, fats) and predicts the optimal diet type with a machine learning model loaded at startup (diet_model.pkl), exposed via a CORS-enabled Flask API.

Calculation Methods

BMR Calculation

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), which represents the number of calories your body needs at rest.

Male: BMR = 10 √ó weight(kg) + 6.25 √ó height(cm) - 5 √ó age + 5

Female: BMR = 10 √ó weight(kg) + 6.25 √ó height(cm) - 5 √ó age - 161

TDEE Calculation

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is calculated by multiplying your BMR by an activity multiplier based on your workout frequency:

  • 0-1 days/week: 1.2 (Sedentary)
  • 2-3 days/week: 1.375 (Light exercise)
  • 4-5 days/week: 1.55 (Moderate exercise)
  • 6-7 days/week: 1.65 (Heavy exercise)

Goal Adjustments

TDEE is adjusted based on your fitness goal:

  • Weight Loss: 15% calorie deficit
  • Muscle Gain: 15% calorie surplus
  • Maintain: No adjustment

Macronutrient Distribution

Macronutrients are calculated based on your goal:

  • Protein: 1.6-2.0 g/kg (varies by goal)
  • Fats: 25-30% of total calories
  • Carbs: Remaining calories after protein and fats
